About Me

My name is Nirmal. I am 15 years old and currently studying in Grade 9. I am from the Morang District of Nepal, a place that has shaped much of who I am. I have many hobbies and interests that I enjoy in my free time, and they help me learn, stay creative, and express myself better. Through this portfolio, I want to share my background, the things I enjoy doing, and the experiences that make me who I am.

My life and goals

I was born in Damak, Nepal, but my ancestral home is in Rangeli. I spent my first year living in my ancestral home before moving to Butwal, where I lived for three years. When I was four, my family shifted to Pokhara, and that’s where I spent the next ten years studying and creating countless memories. Pokhara holds a special place in my heart—the laughter with friends, school adventures, and all the little moments that made my childhood unforgettable. Last year, while in Grade 8, I had the chance to revisit my birth district, which was both nostalgic and exciting. Looking ahead, my dream is to become an IT engineer and move to America to live there happily with my family.

My hobbies and interests

Watching anime is one of my favorite hobbies. I enjoy it because anime combines beautiful art, interesting characters, and powerful stories that keep me entertained for hours. Playing Video Games

Playing video games is a hobby that I really enjoy in my free time. Playing football
