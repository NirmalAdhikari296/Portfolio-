MO:MO Recipe

Ingredients

  • All-purpose flour
  • Salt
  • Water
  • Ground/Minced Meat
  • Onion
  • Cabbage
  • Ginger and Garlic
  • Chili
  • Momo Masala or Garam Masala
  • Salt and Pepper
  • Oil

Recipe

  1. Mix all-purpose flour with a pinch of salt and add water gradually to knead a smooth dough.
  2. Cover the dough with a damp cloth and let it rest for 20–30 minutes.
  3. In a bowl, combine ground meat with finely chopped onion, cabbage, ginger, garlic, chili, salt, pepper, momo masala (or garam masala), and a little oil. Mix well.
  4. Divide the dough into small balls and roll each one into a thin round wrapper.
  5. Place a spoonful of filling in the center of each wrapper.
  6. Fold and pleat the edges carefully to seal the momo.
  7. Lightly oil the steamer tray and arrange the momos with some space between them.
  8. Steam over boiling water for 10–12 minutes until cooked.
  9. Serve hot with achar or chutney.
